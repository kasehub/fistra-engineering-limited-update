Fire Engineering

Our fire engineering services focus on protecting people, property, and the environment from the devastating effects of fire. We employ performance-based design approaches that go beyond prescriptive code requirements to create innovative and effective fire safety solutions.

With expertise in fire dynamics, human behavior, and building systems, we develop comprehensive fire safety strategies tailored to each project's unique requirements.

Key Services:

  • Fire Safety Design - passive and active protection systems
  • Fire Risk Assessment - hazard identification and safety audits
  • Evacuation Modelling & Planning - simulation of people movement
  • Fire Suppression & Detection Systems design
  • Performance-based design approaches
  • Training & Capacity Building in Fire Safety
