Kazungula Bridge

2019 - 2022
Zambia-Botswana Border
Bridge Engineering
Infrastructure

The Kazungula Bridge is an extradosed bridge that spans the Zambezi River between Zambia and Botswana. With a total length of 923 meters and a main span of 129 meters, it accommodates both road and rail traffic, significantly improving transportation links in the region.

As Bridge Engineer and QA Engineer for Daewoo E&C, our Managing Director was responsible for quality assurance, design verification, and ensuring compliance with international standards throughout the construction phase.

Key Features

  • Extradosed bridge design combining cable-stayed and girder bridge elements
  • Dual-purpose structure accommodating both highway and railway
  • Advanced seismic considerations for the region
  • International collaboration between multiple engineering firms
  • Environmental considerations for the Zambezi River ecosystem

Technical Specifications

Total Length: 923 meters | Main Span: 129 meters | Structure Type: Extradosed Bridge | Materials: Prestressed concrete and steel
