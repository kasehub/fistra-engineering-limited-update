Allplan

Integrated BIM solution for architecture and engineering. Allplan enables seamless collaboration between different disciplines and facilitates detailed 3D modeling of complex structural elements.

Advanced structural analysis and design software for buildings and civil engineering structures. We utilize Scia Engineer for its efficient workflow and comprehensive code-checking capabilities.

Visual programming language and environment that runs within Rhino. We use Grasshopper for parametric design, generative algorithms, and complex geometric modeling for innovative structural solutions.

Versatile 3D modeling software used for free-form surfaces, organic shapes, and complex geometries. Rhino enables us to create and analyze sophisticated structural forms with precision.

BIM software for steel and concrete detailing, fabrication, and construction management. We use Tekla for accurate 3D modeling of structural components and seamless collaboration with fabricators.

Integrated structural analysis and design software for concrete and steel structures. ProtaStructure facilitates rapid modeling, analysis, and detailing within a single BIM environment.
